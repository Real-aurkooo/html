Bangladesh, officially the People's Republic of Bangladesh, is a South Asian nation known for its lush landscapes, vibrant culture, and rich history. Bordered by India and Myanmar, it's a densely populated country, famous for the Sundarbans mangrove forest and its delta region. Bangladesh gained independence in 1971 after a liberation war and is a developing nation striving for progress in various sectors, including technology and education
